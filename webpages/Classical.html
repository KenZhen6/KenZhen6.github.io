Classical Music

Classical music is a type of music which was produced in the earlier eras of music (Mainly the Classical period). This article will give insight on the classical art that is "Classical Music".

The years of the Classical period dates around the years of 1730 and the year 1820. Scientists are unsure however know that the classical period was between the baroque and romantic periods which shows it must have been between the end date of the baroque period and the start of the romantic period.

Music from the classical period had a very unique style, it was formed from a lot of different types of instruments, violins being one of the most popular ones.

Ludwig van Beethoven

A picture (top) of Beethoven

Ludwig van Beethoven was a Classical music composer. Said to be one of the greatest composers of classical music ever, he made many popular classical pieces which are still acknowledged to this day.

He was born on the 16th, December 1770, in the city Bonn in Germany.

He died on the 26th of March, 1827 in Vienna, Austria.
